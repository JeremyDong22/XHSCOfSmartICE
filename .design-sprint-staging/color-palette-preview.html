Softer Dark Mode Philosophy

This version uses Tailwind's Stone palette for warmer, less harsh dark backgrounds: Stone 900 (#1C1917) as base, Stone 800 (#292524) for elevated surfaces. The warm undertones reduce eye strain while maintaining the sophisticated Anthropic aesthetic. Terracotta (#D97757) remains the signature accent color.
